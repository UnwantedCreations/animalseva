Who We Are

Dedicated to the Wellbeing of All Sentient Beings

The Animal Seva Foundation was born from a simple belief: every animal deserves dignity. We focus heavily on Gau Seva (Cow Service), ensuring that abandoned and injured cows find sanctuary.

Our team works round the clock to rescue accident victims, treat mange and wounds in street dogs, and provide daily meals to hundreds of stray animals.

24/7 Emergency Rescue
Daily Feeding Drives
Medical Treatment & Surgery
Our Impact

Areas of Seva

Gau Seva

Providing shelter, fodder, and medical aid to old, abandoned, and injured cows. We ensure they live their lives in peace.

Street Dog Care

Treating accident cases, skin infections, and sterilization. We also run daily feeding programs for community dogs.

Medical & Cat Aid

Specialized care for cats and other small animals. Our ambulance is equipped to provide on-spot first aid.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Transparency in every transaction.

1. General Policy

Animal Seva Foundation is a non-profit organization, and we rely on the generosity of our donors. As a general rule, donations once made cannot be refunded. However, we understand that errors can happen.

2. Duplicate Transactions

If you have made a duplicate donation due to a technical glitch or error, please contact us immediately at help@animalseva.org with proof of the transaction. We will verify the claim and refund the duplicate amount within 7-10 working days.

3. Unauthorized Transactions

If you suspect that your card or account has been used to make a donation without your authorization, please contact your bank immediately. We will cooperate fully with the bank's investigation.

4. Cancellation of Monthly Subscriptions

If you have subscribed to our monthly donation program, you can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting our support team or logging into your donor dashboard. The cancellation will be effective from the next billing cycle.

5. 80G Tax Exemption

Once a donation receipt is issued for tax exemption purposes under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, we are unable to process a refund for that specific donation.
